Here are some links to get you started:

  • ./sendECO2ToServer
    • An endpoint that receives co2 data; arguments= "co2": float; type: POST
    You can connect this one to your m5stack to send data to the server.

  • ./getWeatherData
    • An endpoint to retreive weather data; arguments: None; type: GET
    You can connect this one to your m5stack to retreive weather data from the server. The temperature is in celcius and the CO2 is in ppm.
    The temperature is taken from the open-meteo API and the CO2 is taken from the m5stack.
